Who are we?

Prestine isn't just a group, we are a collective of driven individuals, united by a shared passion for creating positive change within our community. Born from the initiative of two dedicated representatives from BA Psychology 201 at STI Ortigas-Cainta Campus, our team quickly expanded to embrace additional students, all eager to contribute their unique skills and talents to a common goal promoting cleanliness and environmental responsibility.

The success of our "Pamilya Mission: Clean Possible!" project wasn't just about the distribution of essential cleaning materials; it was about cultivating a sense of community and fostering a lasting commitment to environmental stewardship. Our interactive activities and engaging presentations transcended the mere donation of supplies. The smiles on the faces of the families we served, the palpable sense of accomplishment amongst our team, and the knowledge that we had successfully reached 25 households, are the true measures of our success. Prestine is not merely defined by the tasks completed, but by the impact we made. We are the embodiment of collaborative action, turning a shared vision into a tangible reality.

MISSION:
To inspire every individual to be committed to creating a clean, sustainable, and healthy environment.

VISION:
To be a prime mover in building a harmonious community that values tidiness and is committed to eco-driven action.

Our Skills and Abilities

Management: We successfully planned and managed a large-scale community event, including securing approvals, managing a budget, and coordinating multiple teams and resources. This involved meticulous planning, clear communication, and effective problem-solving.

Teamwork and Collaboration: We effectively collaborated with representatives from other courses, demonstrating excellent collaboration and communication skills. Internal team coordination was also vital for our success, as evidenced by our coordinated purchasing, packaging, and event execution.

Logistics and Operations: We effectively handled all aspects of logistics, including purchasing supplies, managing transportation for 50 participants, and organizing the event venue.

Community Engagement: We successfully engaged 50 participants in our event through well-planned activities and an engaging presentation. This shows an understanding of community needs and effective community outreach

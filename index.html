Pamilya Mission: Clean Possible!

The “Pamilya Mission: Clean Possible!” was a donation drive conducted on Friday, April 28, 2025, from 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM at Exodus Floodway in Taytay, Rizal. This project mainly provided families essential cleaning materials that purposely sustain a safe and healthy environment. Besides, this project advocated cleanliness, environmental responsibility and awareness, and built up parent-child relationships. It encouraged participants to assimilate cleaning practices that particularly impact their household and the community as well. To make sure of its effectiveness, the representatives showcased different interactive activities and performance. These demonstrated the importance of understanding teamwork and cleanliness. The initiative empowered the participants to take their responsibilities in nurturing the environment and maintaining cleanliness.
